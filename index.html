KochChart.com

Welcome! This site is dedicated to helping pilots understand the effects of density altitude on takeoff performance using the Koch Chart. This is a free, educational tool created to raise awareness about aircraft performance limitations—especially for pilots flying at high-elevation airports.

Stay safe and enjoy exploring the Koch Chart!
